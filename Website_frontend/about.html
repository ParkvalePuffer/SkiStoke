Our Technology

SkiStoke leverages advanced weather APIs and meteorological models to provide accurate snow forecasts. Our system:

  • Uses elevation-specific data - We pull forecasts at actual resort elevations for maximum accuracy
  • Implements fallback systems - When direct snow data isn't available, we use temperature and precipitation models
  • Provides visual indicators - Color-coded bars help you quickly assess snow quality
  • Updates continuously - Data refreshes automatically to keep you informed
